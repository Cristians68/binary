Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

B1nary Academy ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the B1nary Academy mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: Name and email address when you create an account.
  • Usage data: Course progress, quiz scores, lesson completions, badges earned, and streak data.
  • Device data: Device type, operating system version, and app version for debugging purposes.
  • Purchase data: In-app purchase records processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalise your learning experience.
  • Track your progress across courses and modules.
  • Award badges and maintain streak records.
  • Send push notifications about your learning streak and new content (only if you grant permission).
  • Improve the app based on aggregated usage data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which is hosted in the United States. Firebase complies with GDPR and other major data protection regulations. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and cloud database.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management.
  • Apple App Store — payment processing.

Each service has its own privacy policy. We do not sell your data to advertisers or any third parties.

Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications to remind you about your learning streak or to notify you of new content. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings or within the B1nary app under Profile → Notifications.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Delete your account at any time from within the app under Profile → Delete Account.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time.

Children's Privacy

B1nary Academy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
