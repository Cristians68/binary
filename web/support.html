Common Questions

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?
Open the app, go to Profile → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to buy.

I want a refund — what do I do?
All in-app purchases are processed by Apple. Refund requests must be submitted to Apple directly at reportaproblem.apple.com. We can't issue refunds on Apple's behalf.

How do I delete my account?
In the app, go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.

I'm not getting push notifications
Check that notifications are enabled both in your device Settings (Settings → Notifications → B1nary) and in the app under Profile → Notifications.

The app is crashing or behaving oddly
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, please email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what you were doing when it happened.

Reporting Bugs

Found something broken? Email support@binaryapp.org with:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • A screenshot if you have one

Course Content Questions

If you spot an error in a flashcard or quiz question, or have feedback on course content, please let us know at support@binaryapp.org. We review all feedback and update content regularly.

Response Time

We typically respond to support emails within 1-2 business days. Thanks for your patience!
